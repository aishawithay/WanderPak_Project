Islamabad

Islamabad, Pakistan's capital, stands as a modern testament to architectural elegance, with its sweeping vistas and orderly streets. Nestled against the backdrop of the Margalla Hills, it offers a harmonious blend of tradition and progress.

Islamabad, known as the "Green City," captivates with its dynamic energy, from the iconic Faisal Mosque to bustling markets, offering a serene escape amidst abundant green spaces.

Accommodation

There are plenty of accommodation options in Islamabad, ranging from budget hotels to luxury resorts. Some popular options include:

  • Serena Hotel Islamabad
  • Margala Hotel Islamabad
  • Hillview Hotel Islamabad

Expected Price Range

The price range for accommodation in Islamabad varies depending on the location and the type of accommodation. On average, budget hotels can cost between $50 to $100 per night, while luxury resorts can cost upwards of $150 per night.
