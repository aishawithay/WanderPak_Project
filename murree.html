Murree

Murree, often referred to as the "Queen of the Hills," is a scenic hill station located in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It has become one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country.

Murree is renowned for its pleasant climate, stunning landscapes, and vibrant atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy the breathtaking views of the snow-capped mountains, explore historical sites, and experience the rich cultural heritage that Murree has to offer.

Accommodation

There are plenty of accommodation options in Murree, ranging from budget guesthouses to luxury resorts. Some popular options include:

  • Pearl Continental Bhurban
  • Shangrila Resort Hotel
  • Hotel One Mall Road

Expected Price Range

The price range for accommodation in Murree varies depending on the location and the type of accommodation. On average, budget guesthouses can cost between $20 to $40 per night, while luxury resorts can cost upwards of $100 per night.
